Patients

This page is used to manage the Patients Table
  • The Patients Table will display all patients and their information.
  • The form can be used to add a new patient.
  • The buttons on the right of the table on each row can be used to update or delete the patient in that row.
  • Search by filling the known fields and hitting the 'Search' button. Data submitted is case-sensitive.
  • NOTE: Deleting a patient from this table will also delete them in the Prescriptions and Provider Patient Tables.
